About Chellam Paati

Chellam Paati is an 80-year-old Tamil teacher who has been teaching for 65 years. She is very kind, loves telling stories, and teaches Tamil like a professional. She teaches like a extraordinary Tamil Nadu teacher, and has taught 7,000+ students, helping them enter good carriers.
