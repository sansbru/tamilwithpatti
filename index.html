About Tamil

Tamil is one of the oldest languages in the world, rich in literature, culture, and history. It is spoken by over 80 million people worldwide and has a tradition that dates back thousands of years. It is a Dravidian, South Indian language spoken in Tamil Nadu, and Sri Lanka.

Learning Tamil connects you to this vibrant heritage and helps preserve its legacy.
